Our Story

Crafting unforgettable journeys through the mystical landscapes of Eastern India and Sikkim since 2018.

How Travelogue Began

What started as a passionate group of local explorers has blossomed into the region's most trusted travel agency. We realized that true travel isn't just about visiting a place; it's about connecting with its soul.

Our mission is to unveil the hidden gems of Sikkim, Darjeeling, Sitong, the Dooars, and Mandarmani while promoting sustainable tourism that uplifts local communities.

Our Vision

To be the bridge between curious travelers and the untouched beauty of the Himalayas, ensuring every journey leaves a positive footprint.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every itinerary we craft.

Passion

We love what we do, and it reflects in every trip we organize.

Integrity

Honest pricing and transparent policies. No hidden surprises.

Sustainability

Protecting the fragile ecosystem of the hills, forests, and coast for future generations.

Excellence

Delivering premium, polished experiences from arrival to departure.
