Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, make a booking, or contact our support team. This may include:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Travel Information: Passport details (if required for permits), dietary requirements, and emergency contacts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your bookings and arrange necessary travel permits.
  • Communicate with you regarding your itinerary, updates, and customer support.
  • Improve our website, services, and overall user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your privacy is important to us. Our website primarily utilizes local storage within your browser to manage sessions securely. Any sensitive data transmitted over the internet is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Hotels, transport providers, and local guides necessary to fulfill your booking.
  • Government Authorities: To procure necessary travel permits (e.g., Inner Line Permits for Sikkim).

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information to improve navigation and user experience.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to ask that your personal data be corrected, updated, or deleted. Please contact us at privacy@travelogue.com for any data-related requests.
