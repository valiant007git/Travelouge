Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you embark on your journey.

Booking & Payments

How do I book a tour package?

You can easily book a package by logging into your Client Dashboard, navigating to the Packages section, and clicking "Book Now". Alternatively, you can use our Plan Trip inquiry form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm). For international clients, we also accept PayPal and Wire Transfers.

Is online payment secure?

Absolutely. All transactions are processed through 256-bit encrypted gateways that comply with PCI-DSS standards. We never store your card details on our servers.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, for bookings made at least 60 days in advance, you can secure your reservation with a 30% deposit. The remaining 70% must be paid 15 days prior to the departure date.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before travel will receive a 90% refund. Cancellations made 15-29 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable.

Travel & Destinations

What is the best time to visit Sikkim?

The best time to visit Sikkim is either during Spring (March to May) when the rhododendrons are blooming, or Autumn (September to November) for clear skies and the best views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Do I need a permit for Sikkim?

Yes, foreign nationals require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to enter Sikkim. Indian nationals require Protected Area Permits (PAP) for certain areas like Nathula Pass and North Sikkim. We arrange all permits for our clients.

Is Darjeeling safe for solo female travelers?

Yes, Darjeeling is widely considered one of the safest destinations in India for solo female travelers. The locals are hospitable, crime rates are very low, and the environment is highly tourist-friendly.

What should I pack for a hill station trip?

Layered clothing is key. Bring thermal wear, a good quality windproof/waterproof jacket, comfortable walking/trekking shoes, sunglasses, sunscreen, and essential personal medications.

Are vegetarian meals available?

Absolutely! All our partnered hotels and homestays provide excellent vegetarian options. Vegan and Jain meal requests can also be accommodated if informed in advance.

Accommodation

What types of accommodation do you offer?

We offer everything from budget-friendly eco-stays and authentic local homestays to luxury 5-star heritage resorts, depending on your selected package and budget.

Are homestays safe and comfortable?

Yes, we personally verify every homestay we partner with. They offer modern amenities, clean attached bathrooms, and incredible local hospitality that hotels simply cannot match.

Can I request a specific hotel?

If you are booking a Custom Tour, you can absolutely request specific properties. For fixed packages, we provide a list of our standard partner hotels of equivalent categories.

Group & Custom Tours

Can you arrange custom private tours?

Yes! Our travel experts specialize in crafting bespoke itineraries. Simply fill out the "Plan Trip" form with your requirements and we will design a trip just for you.

Do you offer group discounts?

We offer special group rates for parties of 8 or more travelers. Please contact our support team directly for group quotes and corporate bookings.

Can I modify the itinerary?

Custom tours can be modified completely before booking. Fixed packages have some flexibility for minor tweaks, subject to availability and additional costs.
