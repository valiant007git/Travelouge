Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Travelogue. These Terms & Conditions govern your use of our website and services. By booking a tour with us, you agree to be bound by these terms. Please read them carefully.

2. Booking & Payments

  • A deposit of 30% of the total tour cost is required to secure your booking.
  • The remaining balance must be paid at least 15 days prior to the departure date.
  • For bookings made within 15 days of departure, full payment is required immediately.
  • Prices are quoted in INR (Indian Rupees) and are subject to change without prior notice until the booking is confirmed.

3. Cancellations & Refunds

If you need to cancel your booking, the following cancellation fees apply:

  • 30 days or more prior to departure: 10% of total tour cost
  • 15 to 29 days prior to departure: 50% of total tour cost
  • 14 days or less prior to departure: 100% of total tour cost (No Refund)

Refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days via the original method of payment.

4. Itinerary Changes

Travelogue reserves the right to modify the itinerary due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, road closures, or political unrest. In such cases, we will provide alternative arrangements of a similar standard.

5. Liability

Travelogue acts only as an agent for the hotels, transport providers, and other contractors. We shall not be held responsible for any loss, injury, or damage to person or property in connection with any accommodation, transportation, or other services.

6. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that all clients purchase comprehensive travel insurance covering trip cancellation, medical expenses, and loss of baggage before departing on their tour.
