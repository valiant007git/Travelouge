Mandarmani

A polished coastal escape with premium resorts, quiet beach time, and easy access from Kolkata.

About Mandarmani

Mandarmani is one of Bengal's easiest luxury coastal resets: long beach walks, seafood lunches, quiet resort afternoons, and relaxed family-friendly pacing. It pairs beautifully with Dooars or Darjeeling for travelers who want a forest-to-coast itinerary.

Best Time To Visit

October to March brings the most comfortable weather. Winter weekends are lively, while weekdays are best for travelers who prefer a calmer, premium beach stay.

Top Experiences

Slow Beach Mornings

Early walks, soft light, and unhurried resort breakfasts.

Premium Resort Stays

Comfortable rooms, pools, lawns, and sea-facing upgrades where available.

Seafood Trail

Fresh local plates with relaxed lunch stops along the route.

Coastal Drive

A comfortable private transfer from Kolkata with planned refreshment stops.

Sample Itinerary (3D/2N)

Day 1: Kolkata to Mandarmani

Private pickup, coastal drive, resort check-in, and a relaxed sunset walk by the beach.

Day 2: Leisure, Beach, and Seafood

Slow breakfast, beach time, optional Tajpur visit, seafood lunch, and an evening by the resort lawn or pool.

Day 3: Departure

Morning at leisure before checkout and a private transfer back to Kolkata.
