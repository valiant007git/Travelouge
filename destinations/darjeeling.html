Darjeeling

The Queen of the Hills & Home of the World Famous Tea

About Darjeeling

Darjeeling, affectionately known as the 'Queen of the Hills', offers breathtaking vistas of Mount Kanchenjunga, lush green tea gardens rolling down the mountainsides, and a charming colonial heritage. From the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train) to the spectacular sunrise at Tiger Hill, it is the perfect romantic getaway and family destination.

Best Time To Visit

September to December for the clearest views of Kanchenjunga, and March to May for pleasant summer weather.

Top Attractions

Tiger Hill

Famous for spectacular sunrise over Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Happy Valley Tea Estate

Experience the processing of world-class Darjeeling tea.

Batasia Loop

A spiral railway track with a panoramic view of Darjeeling.

Chowrasta (Mall Road)

The bustling heart of Darjeeling, perfect for evening walks.

Local Food Highlights

  • Darjeeling Tea: The 'Champagne of Teas'.
  • Keventers Breakfast: Iconic sausages and hot chocolate.
  • Alu Dum: Spicy local potato curry.

Sample Itinerary (3D/2N)

Day 1: Arrival & Local Walk

Arrive at Bagdogra/NJP and transfer to Darjeeling (3 hours). Check-in and relax. In the evening, take a stroll down the famous Mall Road (Chowrasta) and enjoy the colonial architecture. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 2: Tiger Hill & Sightseeing

Wake up at 4 AM to visit Tiger Hill for the majestic sunrise over Kanchenjunga. On the way back, visit Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop. Post breakfast, visit the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Zoo, and a Tea Garden. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some last-minute shopping for Darjeeling Tea. Proceed to Bagdogra/NJP for your onward journey.
