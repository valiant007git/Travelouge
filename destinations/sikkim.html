Sikkim

The Himalayan Wonderland of Monasteries & Glaciers

About Sikkim

Nestled in the Himalayas, Sikkim is a sanctuary of profound peace, cascading waterfalls, and untouched alpine landscapes. Home to Kangchenjunga, the highest peak in India, it offers an incredible mix of adventure, Buddhist culture, and rare biodiversity. Whether you seek the spiritual calm of ancient monasteries or the adrenaline rush of high-altitude treks, Sikkim is a destination that stays with you long after you leave.

Best Time To Visit

The optimal windows are Spring (March to May) for blooming orchids and rhododendrons, and Autumn (September to November) for clear views of the Himalayan range.

Top Attractions

Tsomgo Lake

A glacial lake at 12,400 ft, known for its changing colors.

Rumtek Monastery

The largest monastery in Sikkim, rich in Tibetan architecture.

Nathula Pass

A historic mountain pass on the Indo-China border at 14,140 ft.

Yumthang Valley

The 'Valley of Flowers', spectacular in the spring.

Local Food Highlights

  • Momos: Steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.
  • Thukpa: A hearty Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Gundruk: Fermented leafy greens, a traditional staple.

Sample Itinerary (4D/3N)

This is a standard flow. Customizations are available upon booking.

Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok

Arrive at Bagdogra Airport (IXB) or NJP Railway Station. Our representative will meet you and transfer you to Gangtok (approx 4 hours). Check in to your hotel and spend the evening exploring the famous MG Marg. Enjoy local street food and shopping. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Meals: Dinner

Day 2: Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

After breakfast, start for a full day excursion to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft) and Baba Mandir (13,200 ft). The lake is considered sacred by the locals. You can also opt for a ride to Nathula Pass (subject to permit availability). Return to Gangtok by evening. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 3: Gangtok Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a half-day local city tour. Visit Rumtek Monastery, Dro-dul Chorten, Research Institute of Tibetology, Directorate of Handicraft & Handloom, and the Flower Show. Afternoon free for leisure. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Bagdogra Airport / NJP Station for your onward journey with beautiful memories of Sikkim.

Meals: Breakfast
